-   **Human-in-the-Loop (HITL)**: A mandatory human review step is at the core of the architecture. The system tracks AI suggestions, user answers, and corrections.

-   **Backend-Driven UI State**: The logic for applicability and question generation resides on the backend (), ensuring a single source of truth, while the frontend provides real-time reactivity.
