**Key Technical Concepts**
-   **Line-by-Line Segmentation**: The architectural shift from classifying an entire document to analyzing each line item individually. This is the core logic in  and is triggered for MISTA (mixed) or borderline cases.
-   **Eligibility Gates**: The use of a strict, multi-point checklist of deterministic rules () to decide if a subsequent automated workflow (like Phase 2) can be safely triggered. This prevents automation on ambiguous or incomplete data.
-   **Flexible Scoring**: The validation scoring logic () was enhanced with more flexible string matching (checking for substrings, handling synonyms) to better reflect the AI's actual performance, which was crucial for improving the measured extraction score.
-   **Ground Truth Correction**: A key insight from the validation process was that the initial ground truth was sometimes incorrect. The failed AI classifications were often correctly identifying mixed cases that had been oversimplified in the test data.
